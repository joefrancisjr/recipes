Ingredients

- 1 bag of elbow macaroni
- 1 bottle of tomato juice, low sodium
- 1 pound of sharp cheddar cheese

Instructions

- boil macaroni until soft
- grate cheese
- mix 3/4 of cheese into macaroni with tomato juice in a glass casserole dish
- sprinkle remaining cheese on top of pasta
- bake at 425 degrees for 30 minutes
- broil for 10 minutes
- cool 10 minutes
- serve!
